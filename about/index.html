About

A long-term archive built for clarity, continuity, and practical navigation.

The Brief

  • Build systems that are easy to navigate and hard to lose.
  • Respect the reader with clean markup and accessible structure.
  • Keep the content canonical, linkable, and durable.

Editorial Method

  • Interviews and videos are indexed by stable IDs so links and metadata stay durable.
  • Generated indexes are rebuilt from canonical data files, not hand-edited page fragments.
  • Transcript quality and metadata completeness are monitored as operational work, not one-time cleanup.
  • Curation tracks are explicit about scope and sequence so the archive reads as lineage, not a dump.
