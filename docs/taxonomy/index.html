Taxonomy

A simple, durable classification system for interviews, oneoffs, and SCMC videos.

Interviews

  • Primary archive of interviews tied to canonical video assets.
  • Associated with a conference or community when known.
  • Uses video_asset_id as the stable link for embeds.

Oneoffs

  • Standalone talks, experiments, and non-interview videos.
  • Often single-platform; still tracked in video_assets.
  • Skate videos are treated as oneoffs.

SCMC

  • Software Craftsmanship McHenry County recordings.
  • Curated list referencing video_assets.
