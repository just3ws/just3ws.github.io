# Refined Content Strategy: Technical Engineering Archive

## 1. Executive Summary
The mission is to transform the 183-item technical interview archive from a static repository into a **Technical Authority Engine**. By positioning the archive as the canonical record of the Software Craftsmanship movement and its Chicago roots, we establish Mike Hall as a primary curator of industry wisdom.

## 2. Refined Content Pillars

| Pillar | Rationale | Connection to Product |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Architectural Archeology** | Analyzes the 'Why' behind past technical decisions (Clojure, 8th Light, NetBSD). | Direct usage of `insights` and restructured `turns`. |
| **The Chicago Renaissance** | Historical documentation of the SC epicenter (Alt.NET, SCNA). | Leverages the Chicago Hub and local community interviews. |
| **The Founders Series** | Direct technical and ethical wisdom from Agile Manifesto signatories. | Direct usage of the Signatories Hub. |
| **Forensic Engineering** | The meta-narrative of using AI to preserve technical memory. | Builds trust in the data's integrity and Mike's current expertise. |
