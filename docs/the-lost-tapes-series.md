# 'The Lost Tapes' Social Media Campaign

## Campaign Goal
Drive technical authority and traffic to the forensic archive by sharing high-fidelity technical insights from the creators of the modern stack.

## Target Platforms
- LinkedIn (Primary: Thought Leadership)
- X/Twitter (Secondary: Tech Community/Real-time)

## Content Atoms (The Template)
**Hook:** A bold claim or surprising historical context from the interview.
**The Insight:** A 2-3 sentence technical lesson (durable).
**The Archive Link:** Direct link to the structured transcript.
**Visual:** Video thumbnail (if available) or the "Curiosity Lead" description.
**Hashtags:** #TheLostTapes #SoftwareCraftsmanship #TechHistory #EngineeringLeadership
