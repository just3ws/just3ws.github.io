Interviews Conversations on Craft

A decade of dialogues with the engineers, authors, and community builders who shaped the modern software landscape.

This archive preserves nearly 200 conversations recorded at the inflection points of the Ruby and Software Craftsmanship movements. These aren't just technical interviews; they are snapshots of how we thought about architecture, community, and the ethics of building software during a transformative era.

Archive Highlights:

  • Voices of Influence: Featuring Dave Thomas, Sandro Mancuso, Dean Wampler, and others.
  • Core Themes: Deep dives into community building, the evolution of Rails, and the discipline of software craftsmanship.
  • Searchable History: Over 90 conversations include full transcripts for technical reference.
