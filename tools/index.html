Tools

Tools are grouped by purpose so archive operations stay separate from strategic/economic modeling.

Archive Operations

  • Build pipeline scripts for generating interview and video pages.
  • Normalization helpers for metadata, slugs, and canonical IDs.
  • Maintenance scripts for pruning and auditing data quality.
  • Archive Status surfaces current quality signals from generated data.

Strategy and Economics

  • DevEx Funnel Cost Calculator models lifecycle friction costs from planning through offboarding and translates delivery signals into executive-facing cost context.
