I’m looking forward to attending my first Chicago ALT.NET meeting this Wednesday.  The topic is Cruise, the new continuous integration server being released by ThoughtWorks Studio.

More information can be found on Sergio Pereira’s blog.

Off the top of my head one question probably will be regarding whether the development on Cruise will affect the development and support of the current open-source CruiseControl.NET system that is also by ThoughtWorks.

Meeting Info:

Chicago ALT.NET Users Group Meeting
Wednesday, August 13th @ 6:00 PM CST

The meeting location is at ThoughtWorks, 200 East Randolph Street, 25th Floor, Chicago, Illinois 60611.

Visit Eventbrite to register: http://altnetchicago.eventbrite.com/

If you’re a suburbanite like myself and happen to be taking the Metra/Union Pacific Northwest, you can catch the outbound 659 train at 8:30 PM that will put you in Crystal Lake at around 9:51 PM.  It’s no worse than driving!
