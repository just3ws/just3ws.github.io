This is the laziest blog post of the month. I’ve been completely distracted this afternoon playing around with IronRuby. Last night I got Ruby on Rails running on the latest release of IronRuby. Anyway, having lots of fun with IronRuby while my daughter naps! It’s definitely a hot language, so hot that working in it makes my poor laptop’s fan spin like crazy.
