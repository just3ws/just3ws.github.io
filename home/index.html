Welcome. I’m Mike Hall.

Staff/Principal Software Engineer focused on architecture, resilience, and long-term system stewardship.

I work in complex, legacy-constrained environments where reliability, risk, and delivery all matter at the same time. My focus is helping teams stabilize critical systems, modernize platforms in place, and restore the shared understanding needed to make safe, durable change.

Most recently, I held architectural ownership for a customer acquisition platform in a regulated financial environment. I helped restructure failing teams, reduce systemic risk, lead end-of-life remediation, and establish observability practices that stayed healthy after handoff.

My leadership style is practical and collaborative. I stay close to the code, translate technical risk into clear business impact, and build structures that can outlast any one project or person.
